## Pimple Location Analysis

The system analyses pimple location and provides specific reasons and suggestions for each area.

### Forehead
- **Reasons**: Lack of sleep, stress, excess sebum, high sugar intake, dirty glasses or spectacles, heavy hair products (gels, waxes), poor lymphatic drainage, sweat accumulation
- **Suggestions**: Get 7-8 hours of sleep, reduce stress, use oil-free hair products, clean glasses daily, reduce sugar intake, keep hair off forehead

### Cheeks (Left and Right)
- **Reasons**: Dirty pillowcase (bacteria transfer), dirty phone screen, touching face with unwashed hands, hormonal imbalance (androgen fluctuations), non-comedogenic makeup, clogged pores, stress, lack of sleep
- **Suggestions**: Change pillowcases weekly, sanitise phone daily, avoid touching face, use non-comedogenic makeup, remove makeup before sleeping, maintain consistent sleep schedule

### Chin
- **Reasons**: Hormonal imbalance (menstrual cycle fluctuations), digestive system issues, touching chin/resting hands, dirty face masks, poor shaving technique, stress, high sugar intake, excess sebum
- **Suggestions**: Track menstrual cycle, avoid resting chin on hands, change masks daily, use clean razors, reduce sugar intake, use salicylic acid products, consult a gynaecologist if persistent

### Nose
- **Reasons**: Excess sebum (T-zone), clogged pores from oil and dirt, blackheads and whiteheads, frequent touching, sweat, dirty glasses, high sugar intake, poor lymphatic system
- **Suggestions**: Use salicylic acid cleanser, avoid touching nose, use oil-free moisturiser, exfoliate twice weekly, use clay masks, clean glasses frames regularly

### Jawline
- **Reasons**: Hormonal imbalance (androgen fluctuations), stress, touching jaw/resting hands, dirty face masks, sweat, poor lymphatic system, smoking, alcohol consumption
- **Suggestions**: Manage stress through exercise or meditation, avoid touching face, change masks every 4-6 hours, reduce alcohol consumption, quit smoking, consult dermatologist for hormonal acne

### Under Eyes
- **Reasons**: Lack of sleep, stress, eye makeup clogging pores, contact dermatitis from eye products, dirty makeup brushes, allergic reactions, poor lymphatic system
- **Suggestions**: Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep, remove eye makeup before sleeping, use hypoallergenic products, clean makeup brushes after each use, avoid rubbing eyes, use gentle eye cream

### Temples
- **Reasons**: Dirty glasses or sunglasses, sweat from exercise or heat, hair products, clogged pores from sebum, stress, lack of sleep
- **Suggestions**: Clean glasses frames daily, wash face after sweating, use oil-free hair products, keep hair off temples, reduce stress, maintain consistent sleep schedule

### Hairline
- **Reasons**: Hair products (oils and waxes), sweat, dirty hats or headbands, sebum accumulation, lack of proper cleansing along hairline, dead skin cells
- **Suggestions**: Wash hair regularly, avoid heavy products near hairline, clean hats and headbands after each use, cleanse hairline area thoroughly, use oil-free products

### Between Eyebrows
- **Reasons**: Excess sebum, clogged pores from hair products, improper hair removal, ingrown hairs, dirty makeup brushes, stress
- **Suggestions**: Use gentle cleanser, avoid waxing if sensitive, clean eyebrow tools, use salicylic acid spot treatment, wash makeup brushes weekly

## Common Factors and Their Impact

| Factor | Effect on Skin |
|--------|----------------|
| Lack of Sleep | Disrupts skin repair cycle, increases inflammation |
| Stress | Increases cortisol, triggers oil production |
| High Sugar Intake | Causes inflammation, glycation damage |
| Hormonal Imbalance | Triggers androgen-induced oil production |
| Digestive Issues | Affects toxin elimination, skin inflammation |
| Smoking | Reduces blood flow, premature aging |
| Alcohol | Dehydrates skin, increases inflammation |
| Sweat | Creates bacteria-friendly environment |
| Dirty Pillowcase | Transfers bacteria, oil, dead skin cells |
| Dirty Phone | Transfers bacteria to face |
